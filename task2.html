Ivan Mazepa

Further Information

The basic objectives of Mazepa’s policies as Hetman of Ukraine:

  • Unity (or at least a consolidation) of Ukrainian lands — the Hetmanstate (Hetmanshchyna), the territory on the right-bank of the Dnieper River, the Zaporozhian lands, and if possible, also Slobidska Ukraine and that of the Khans, within the framework of a unified and sovereign Ukrainian state under the Hetmanite regime;
  • Establishment of a strong autocratic Hetmanite authority and class state of a European type, with the preservation of the traditional system of Kozak administration.
